Specialty Consignment & Curated Thrift

Owner-curated or consignment-based — sellers bring items in, the shop selects what makes the cut. More selective than traditional thrift, prices are higher but so are the highs. No color tag systems. Great for specific brands, unique clothing, and furniture with real character.

Salvage, Reuse & Building Materials

A completely different category — NOT clothing or housewares. Salvaged architectural elements, building materials, hardware, doors, windows, cabinets, tile, and fixtures from homes being renovated or demolished. Essential for DIY projects, home flipping, or sourcing unique architectural pieces. Mission-driven nonprofit reuse.
